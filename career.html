Lead of Business Development in Europe

03 / 01 / 2020 Remote

We are a leading manufacturer of electric drives (medium and low voltage) and submersible equipment for oil production. The company has been operating in the B2B CIS and Export markets for over 26 years. Today, in addition to development, production and sale, we offer after-sales service for electrical equipment for various sectors of the industry, including oil and heavy industry, energy and public utilities.

add

In recent years, we have been actively expanding our sales markets and opening foreign representative offices. And now it's time for us to create a sales department for the European market.

And for this we need YOU! Lead of Business Development in Europe.

What kind of specialist are we looking for?

  • Who has а higher technical education.
  • Who has experience in active sales to a global market. A positive long-term (10-30 years) experience in sales of electrical equipment (one of following options):
    • from European manufacturers of medium voltage equipment, like Siemens, ABB, Schneider, Danfos, Power Electronics (Spain) or from European offices of Japanese, Taiwanese and American manufacturers, like Toshiba, Yaskawa, Hitachi, Rockwell, Mitsubishi, Delta, and others.
    • from European or Asian low-voltage equipment manufacturers: in addition to those listed above - Electronika Santerno, Omron, Fuji, LG and others
    • from European or American manufacturers or submersible equipment, like Baker Hughes, Schlumberger, Oxford, Borets, Novomet. RMS, Netzsch and others.
  • Who knows the names of real buyers, prices, volumes, etc. Who has connections inside these companies what we are interested in. Has its own authority among buyers. Understands well the needs of customers and the problems that they want to solve.
  • Who has knowledge and a thorough understanding of what he sells. And knows all market aspects of electrical equipment.
  • Who is ready and willing to take responsibility in the sale of products in a new market for the company.
  • Who has experience in organizing a new department and managing a team.

What do we offer:

  • Work in an international manufacturing Company
  • Terms of payment: salary + commission on marginal revenue from sales.
  • For the first time, work from home. Approximately 30-40% of the working time is business trips and meetings with customers. After a while - the organization of the office of the company and work as part of a small team.
  • Work with constant professional challenges, solving complex and challenging tasks.
  • An opportunity to independently:
    • organize the work of the office and realize its managerial potential.
    • develop and implement a sales strategy in the new market for the Сompany.
    • influence your own profits and company profits.
    • influence the strategy and development of the Company not only in the European market.
  • And:
    • to be involved in the creation of an important and necessary product for people, to have an impact on improving product quality and its usefulness to the consumer.
    • bring into the activities of their consumers / customers / partners such opportunities that will enable them to solve their most important tasks.
    • the opportunity to in time say to oneself "this is my brainchild".

Возможности для студентов

Наша корпорация тесно взаимодействует со студентами вузов. Большое количество студентов проходит у нас производственную практику во время написания дипломных работ.

Кроме того, у нас открыта школа профессионалов. Ежегодно на период летних каникул мы нанимаем на работу студентов, тщательно подобрав им команду для развития их профессиональных навыков.

По прохождению школы профессионалов, мы выбираем лучших для трудоустройства на постоянной основе и полный рабочий день.

Первый опыт работы после ВУЗа всегда получить сложно, но с нашей школой профессионалов все в Ваших рука!
