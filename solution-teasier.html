TEasier is an offline program for quick and safe access to your PC, favorable sites and media. With TEasier you minimize your time for logging in

Benefits

No need to remember and enter user names and passwords to your PC, favorite sites and media every time!

All it takes to unlock a session or log in your site and media is to look at the camera of your PC. TEasier unlocks your session within 1 sec, when you appear in front of the camera. And Teasier blocks it, if it is not you!

Spoofing protection

If only someone get your password to your PC, he or she is not be able even to log into the PC, because TEasier prevents it.

Data protection

If you store your credentials to your sites and media in a browser, TEasier can do it in a more reliable way. All your data is stored at your PC, locally, i.e. in a more safe way. And again, there is no access to your credentials for anybody.

Functions

Support of any 2D camera

Auto launch after primary tuning

User-friendly interface

24/7 client support

Personal data protection from unauthorized users including anti-trick system (for spoofing detection via photo)

Virtual camera mode (Triol Camera), enabling simultaneous usage of a single physical camera in several apps

Confidentiality of your personal information (the app operates on-device and does not send your face model data to a server)

Short-cut login by means of an appearance-based recognition
